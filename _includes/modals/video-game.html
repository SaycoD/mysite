Project Red Gorilla

Video Game Development

A video game developed for my capstone, reached finals and placed in 3rd at the Capstone Competition.

An online space shooter with a focus on ability powers that can be selected at game lobby. Its a 3 vs 3 combat game with 2 game modes; Team Death Match and Destroy The Carrier.

Developed using Unity3D Engine, C#, and Photon Networking.
